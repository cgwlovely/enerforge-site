Evidence discipline

Every statement knows what it is

Information inside the platform is always labelled, and the label travels into every report:

Publicly verified

Supported by public records or regulator filings.

Screening interpretation

A reasoned estimate, clearly labelled and never presented as fact.

Owner confirmation required

Cannot be resolved from public information; goes on the data request list.

Unresolved gap

Stays visible. A gap is never silently filled to make a conclusion look complete.

Outputs can support four honest results: continue, redesign, supplement data — or stop. A screen that says “pause” is the platform working, not failing.
