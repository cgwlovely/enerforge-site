Case studies

Worked examples of the Pre-DD screen

Each case runs a public-data industrial energy project through the same Pre-DD screen: approval delta, site suitability, microgrid boundary and commercial / financeability readiness. Some cases focus on financeability; others focus on site, approvals and development risk.

How to read these

These are public-information desktop screens of the F1-F4 Pre-DD method, not commissioned advice, lender due diligence or bank-approved models. Figures and zones are indicative early-stage outputs. Nothing here is legal, financial, tax, engineering or investment advice.
