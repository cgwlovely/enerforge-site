The opportunity is in the messy middle

Industrial energy transition is not only about building more renewable energy.

The interesting part is the messy middle:

  • Weak-grid sites
  • Diesel displacement
  • Customer-side energy savings
  • Storage sizing
  • Contract structures
  • Lender requirements
  • Carbon value
  • Operational reliability

This is where many projects get stuck.

The technology may exist. The customer may want savings. The investor may want a deal. But the project still needs a structure that makes sense for everyone.

That is the space I am exploring with EnerForge — not as a finished platform, and not as a formal consulting firm, but as an independent workflow to test project logic early, share project notes and exchange views with others working on industrial solar + BESS, EaaS and energy project development.

The goal is simple:

Make the first project conversation sharper.
