Project snapshot

Remote mine hybrid-energy screen

Location
Fountain Head / Pine Creek, Northern Territory
Technology
Solar PV + BESS + diesel microgrid
Review type
Independent public-information Pre-DD

Key question

Which hybrid configuration is credible enough to advance to owner data, hourly dispatch, EPC pricing and specialist due diligence?

Key findings

  • S4, an indicative 60% annual-renewable pathway, was selected for further validation rather than adopted as a final design.
  • The mapped PV and BESS areas are preliminary desktop search areas, not selected sites or approved equipment footprints.
  • A modelled 1.30x DSCR constraint is a debt-sizing assumption, not lender approval.
  • Owner data, hourly dispatch, equipment sizing and specialist review remain required.
