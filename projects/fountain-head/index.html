Entity facts

Fountain Head Hybrid Energy — a public-information desktop Pre-DD screen of a diesel–PV–BESS hybrid microgrid opportunity at the Fountain Head gold mine (Fountain Head / Pine Creek, Northern Territory, Australia), covering F1 approval delta, F2 site suitability, F3 microgrid boundary and F4 financeability readiness. Public sources date from May 2021 (draft EIS); the screen was built and published June–July 2026.

Evidence

Every published claim links to at least one evidence object, or is explicitly typed as an assumption or marked unresolved. The full registry with publisher, access dates and quality ratings is available as evidence.json. Key public sources:

  • NT EPA Fountain Head assessment register — project history, EIS documents and assessment material
  • Fountain Head draft EIS main report (May 2021) — site energy use, peak load and diesel fuel figures (s3.8.1.1, Table 3.19)
  • PNX Metals / Sunrise public reporting — disclosed PV and BESS starting configuration (primary disclosure URL not yet captured — marked unresolved in the registry)
  • Global Solar Atlas — regional solar-resource context for the yield assumption
